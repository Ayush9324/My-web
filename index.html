🧠 About Me

I am a passionate VFX artist who brings imagination to life.

With a deep love for visual storytelling and cinematic detail, I specialize in creating compelling visual effects for films, short videos, and digital content.

I've worked with After Effects, Premiere Pro, Photoshop, and Blender. I also build games and websites as side projects.

I'm constantly experimenting with new tools and techniques — blending creativity with technology to push visual boundaries.

Attention: All the videos you see here are quite old. I haven’t uploaded anything new in the past year because I was focused on my 12th-grade studies.
